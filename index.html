About

Researching new ways to control and reconstruct light

I am a PhD student in Electrical Engineering at the University of Colorado Boulder. My research focuses on computational microscopy, wavefront shaping, super-resolution imaging, optical sectioning, and imaging through multimode fibers.

Research areas

Current interests

Speckle Imaging

Statistical reconstruction, super-resolution, and optical sectioning using random illumination patterns.

Digital Holography

Complex-field imaging and computational reconstruction using coherent optical interference.

Multimode Fibers

High-speed wavefront shaping and fluorescence microendoscopy through multimode fibers.

Inverse Problems

Deconvolution, phase retrieval, transmission matrices, and regularized image reconstruction.
