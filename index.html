About

Researching new ways to control and reconstruct light

I am a PhD student in Electrical Engineering at the University of Colorado Boulder. My work combines optical experiments, computational imaging, and image reconstruction to improve resolution, contrast, and imaging speed.

Research

Computational microscopy and wavefront shaping

My research includes speckle illumination microscopy, digital holographic microscopy, multimode-fiber imaging, super-resolution, optical sectioning, and inverse imaging problems.

I work with spatial light modulators, high-speed phase modulators, transmission matrices, statistical reconstruction, deconvolution, and Fourier-optics simulations.
